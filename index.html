About Me

I am Salma Balagi, a visual artist dedicated to exploring the unseen aspects of nature, including microorganisms, fungi, and the beauty of decay. My work brings attention to the delicate processes of growth, transformation, and renewal. Through my creations, I aim to inspire others to appreciate the hidden, yet beautiful, aspects of our world.
