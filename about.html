Salma Balagi is a visual artist whose work explores the intersection of art, nature, and transformation. Her practice includes installation, craft, and bioart, often using recycled materials and organic elements to investigate unseen natural processes. Salma draws inspiration from childhood memories, microorganisms, and the delicate metamorphosis of materials over time. Through her work, she reveals overlooked beauty in decay, growth, and subtle environmental shifts.
